About Flag Riddle Game

Welcome to the Flag Riddle Game! Test your knowledge of world flags and learn about different countries' flags while having fun.

How to Play:

  1. You will be presented with an image of a flag.
  2. Choose the correct country from the list of options provided below the flag.
  3. If you select the correct country, you'll receive a point. If your answer is incorrect, you'll be given another chance.
  4. The game continues with a new flag for you to identify.
  5. See how many flags you can correctly identify and how high of a score you can achieve!

Rules:

  • Each correct answer earns you one point.
  • There is no time limit, so take your time to make the right choice.
  • You can play as many rounds as you like to improve your knowledge of world flags.

Enjoy the Flag Riddle Game and have fun while learning about flags from around the world!
